EnjoySubs — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

What EnjoySubs does

EnjoySubs is a Chrome extension that displays dual-language subtitles on Netflix. It intercepts Netflix's own subtitle responses in your browser and renders a custom overlay on top of the video player.

Data collection

EnjoySubs does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No browsing history, viewing habits, or Netflix account information is collected.
  • No subtitle data is sent to any external server.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is used.

Local storage

EnjoySubs uses Chrome's storage API to save your preferences locally on your device. This includes:

  • Selected subtitle languages and sync offsets
  • Styling options (colors, font size, opacity)
  • Panel position and layout preferences
  • Phone remote room code (if you use the remote feature)

These settings never leave your device and are not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Phone remote feature

If you use the optional phone remote control feature, your device connects to a WebSocket relay server hosted on Render.com. This relay:

  • Forwards playback control messages (play, pause, volume, seek) between your phone and your browser — nothing else.
  • Does not store, log, or inspect message contents.
  • Uses a random room code as a shared key. No account or login is required.
  • Does not transmit subtitle data, viewing history, or personal information.

Permissions explained

  • storage — Save your settings locally on your device.
  • tabs — Detect when you are on a Netflix page so the extension can activate.
  • scripting — Inject the subtitle overlay into Netflix pages.
  • Host permissions (netflix.com) — Required to run on Netflix pages only.
  • Host permissions (enjoysubs-relay.onrender.com) — Connect to the phone remote relay server (optional feature).

Third-party services

EnjoySubs does not use Google Analytics, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking services. The only external service used is the optional phone remote relay described above.
