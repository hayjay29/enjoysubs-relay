EnjoySubs - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

EnjoySubs is a Chrome browser extension that displays two subtitle tracks simultaneously on Netflix, Disney+ (including Hulu-bundle titles viewed through disneyplus.com), Amazon Prime Video, and Coupang Play. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the extension handles, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored entirely on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. It is not transmitted to any external server.

  • Subtitle language preferences — your selected Sub A and Sub B languages per platform
  • Usage tracking — seconds of dual subtitle viewing per week (for free tier limit enforcement)
  • Display preferences — font size, subtitle position, color settings, opacity, and panel layout
  • Premium status — whether you have an active subscription (cached locally)
  • Phone remote room code — a random code used when you pair the optional phone remote

Data Processed but Not Stored

  • Streaming-service subtitle data — EnjoySubs intercepts subtitle responses from the streaming site you are watching (Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, or Coupang Play) in order to display a second subtitle track. This data is processed locally in your browser and is never sent to any external server, stored on disk, or shared with anyone.

Data Handled by Third Parties

  • Payment processing (Premium only) — If you purchase a Premium subscription, payment is handled by ExtensionPay (powered by Stripe). Your email address is collected by ExtensionPay to manage your subscription. We do not store your credit card number, billing address, or financial information. See ExtensionPay's privacy policy and Stripe's privacy policy for details.
  • Phone remote feature (optional) — If you use the phone remote control feature, your browser and phone connect to a WebSocket relay server hosted on Render.com. The relay forwards short playback-control messages (play, pause, seek, volume, subtitle toggle) between your two devices using a randomly generated room code as a shared key. The relay does not require an account, does not log or inspect message contents, and does not transmit subtitle text, viewing history, or personal information. Session data is ephemeral and discarded when you disconnect.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or personal information (except through ExtensionPay if you choose to subscribe)
  • We do not collect your Netflix, Disney+, Hulu, Prime Video, or Coupang Play account information or credentials
  • We do not collect your browsing history or viewing habits
  • We do not collect or store subtitle content from any streaming service
  • We do not track which shows or movies you watch
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking pixels
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties for advertising purposes

Permissions Explained

  • Host permissions (netflix.com, disneyplus.com, primevideo.com, coupangplay.com, and the Prime Video subtitle CDN) — Required to inject the dual subtitle display and control panel on the video pages of those services.
  • Host permissions (enjoysubs-relay.onrender.com) — Required only if you use the optional phone remote feature.
  • storage — Required to save your language preferences, display settings, and usage data locally on your device.
  • scripting — Required to inject the subtitle overlay into streaming pages.

Data Retention

  • Local storage data persists until you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data
  • WebSocket relay session data is deleted immediately on disconnect
  • Payment records are retained by ExtensionPay/Stripe according to their retention policies

Your Rights

  • You can view all locally stored data by visiting chrome://extensions and inspecting the extension's storage
  • You can delete all extension data by uninstalling EnjoySubs
  • You can request deletion of your payment records by contacting ExtensionPay support
  • If you are in the EU, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data under GDPR

Children's Privacy

EnjoySubs does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The extension does not require account creation or personal data input.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
