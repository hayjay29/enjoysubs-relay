2. Description of Service

EnjoySubs is a Chrome browser extension that displays two subtitle tracks simultaneously on supported streaming platforms: Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, and Coupang Play. The extension offers a free tier (Netflix only) and a paid Premium subscription that unlocks all supported platforms and additional features.

3. Free Tier

  • Dual subtitle viewing on Netflix
  • No account or payment required
  • Disney+, Prime Video, and Coupang Play require a Premium subscription

4. Premium Subscription

  • Lifetime: $49.99 one-time payment

Premium unlocks unlimited dual subtitle viewing on all supported streaming platforms (Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, Coupang Play), subtitle resizing, phone remote control, and all future Premium features.
